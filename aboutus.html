About Us

Our Inspiration

The seed was planted for this project from the personal experience of one of our team members. This member struggled finding a sublease arrangement in the year of 2019 and saw first-hand how confusing and disorganized the process is. We know the current process doesn't work well enough for many students who are already spending all of their time focusing on their upcoming exam or their group orject due in a week. We plan to make this process simpler for current and future UW-Madison students since we know firsthand how stressful the process is.

Our Goals

Simply put, our objective is to change how young adults in the Madison area find subleases by creating a site exclusively for UW-Madison students. The stress that comes with the disorganization of the existing process is not necessary and we hope to make things much straightforward with our Madison SubLeases system. Just being able to make finding a sublease easier gives students more time for themselves and higher rates of success with less stress. We hope to be a catalyst for that success.
